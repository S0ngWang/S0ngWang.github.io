Projects

  • Heartbeats Separating

    Heartbeats from multiple people received by geophones merge together. We separate each source by building 3D modeling due to their spatical signatures and sparse frequencies.

    • SenSys 2017.

  • Fatigue Recognition

    Leveraging the high directionality of millimeter-wave signals to lessen the difficulty of monitoring heart rate in the real-world scenario and utilize a multi-resolution frequency filter to distinguish heart signal when people are exercising on an all-in-one trainer.

  • Low-power Modulation for Outdoor VLC System

    Existing lighting infrastructure will build a new world of visual light communication (VLC) systems. The key enabler underly this LED-based system is a FSK-PPM modulation scheme which achieves a big decrease in the average-power requirement.
